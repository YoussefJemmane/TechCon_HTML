History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. What started as a weekend meetup has grown into one of the world's most influential technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually. Over the years, TechCon has been at the forefront of major technological breakthroughs, providing a platform where ideas that shape our digital future are born and nurtured.

Mission

Our mission is to accelerate technological innovation by connecting brilliant minds, fostering collaboration, and providing a platform for groundbreaking ideas to flourish. We believe that the future of technology lies in diverse perspectives, inclusive innovation, and the power of community-driven progress. TechCon 2024 aims to bridge the gap between visionary thinking and practical implementation.

Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential figures in technology. Our speakers have included Nobel laureates, tech CEOs, and pioneering researchers who have shaped the digital landscape.

Dr. Sarah Chen

Former VP of AI at Google, Dr. Chen revolutionized natural language processing and spoke about the future of human-AI collaboration at TechCon 2023.

Marcus Rodriguez

Cybersecurity pioneer and founder of SecureNet Technologies, Marcus shared insights on protecting digital infrastructure in an increasingly connected world.

Dr. Aisha Patel

Leading quantum computing researcher from MIT, Dr. Patel demonstrated breakthrough applications in quantum machine learning that left audiences amazed.
